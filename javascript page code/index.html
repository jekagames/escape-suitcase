THE RITUAL

The lines of the symbols represent the flow of the ritual's magic and this flow must be managed carefully.

Avoid causing the flow of power to rise and fall too sharply in sequence.
The horizons begin and end the ritual, like the sun at dawn and dusk.
The channels of power must not be wholly connected at either the beginning, middle, or end of the ritual.
As it proceeds, the pattern of the ritual loses equilibrium and becomes more chaotic.

Treat the flow of the magic with care. Once you have sorted them, activate them in order.
